Learning Goals & Intended Audience

Learning goals

Improves recognition of sentence structure and how are words are combined to form detailed sentences.

Reinforces vocabulary and synonyms to improve general familiarity with the language.

Strengthens the player's spelling and recognition skills.

Importance of Goals

Without a direct line of communication to a native speaker, learning new languages can be very difficult. However, playing video games in other languages is a generally good replacement as it provides immersion, context, and slang that are accurate to real conversation. However, the player needs to be eased in rather than being thrown into a world full of a foreign language, which is what Scrollscribers aims to do.

Target audience

Age group
Ages 12–16
Educational background
Middle/High School students in introductory or intermediate Spanish
Specific learning needs
For players who struggle to learn languages in a disconnected context and require a living world to directly apply and learn the concepts from.

Audience Tailoring

Difficulty
Turn based gameplay allows infinite time for reflection, with healing spells, NPC guides, and the Grimoire all as a reference in case players are stuck.
Content
Memorization isn't the focus, but recognition is. The words that fit each part of speech are given, but players have to build logical sentences in order to create powerful spells.
Engagement strategies
RPG progression systems via leveling and an exploration aesthetic keep players engaged and wanting to keep playing.
02

Game Concept & Mechanics

Narrative / theme

In the fantasy world of Catalonia, the world's strongest magic is cut off from the rest of the world. An ancient language prevents even the most intelligent sorcerers from obtaining great powers. However, your home is under a great sickness, and the healing spells of old are all that can save you now. Will you be able to recover the scrolls of old and transcribe the language once and for all?

Genre & format

RPG Turn-based combat Browser / Mobile / Desktop native game Singleplayer

Game Visuals

Core mechanics

1
Players combine parts of a sentence in turn based battles in order to create spells. Based on the words chosen, spells have different effects, like flaming barrels, healing bolts, or electric thunderclouds.
2
NPCs are throughout the world that require the player to use their language skills in order to gain an upper hand on future opponents. Knowledge can be the success to battle!
3
RPG leveling mechanics are present in order to encourage the player to engage in battle in order to progress.
4
The Grimoire serves as a limited use reference for the game's vocabulary. Players can access it at anytime during battle, but spend Mana Points for each use.

Learning Theories & Perspectives

Cognitive Perspectives
Elements

Learning is Contextual, Knowledge is constructed, Metacognition, Motivation

Cognitive and Constructive Mechanics

Players are encouraged to experiment with new spell combinations and learn which ones are effective against what kind of enemy, and the order in which they should be used via the spell fatigue system. The spells they use in battle are alluded to by NPCs that provide context in how they are used in sentences.

Situated Learning
Elements

Learning In Context, Legitimate Peripheral Participation

Situated Learning Mechanics

The world around the player and most of its NPCs are presented in Spanish, providing a context in which the player is surrounded by native speakers, as if they were actually traveling abroad. All spells are also available to the player, however their usefulness is only provided via convening with more knowledgable citizens to learn which enemies and situations they are effective against, and how those words (and their synonyms) are used in context.

Behaviorist Principles
Elements

Classical Conditioning, Positive / Negative reinforcement, Conditioning, Repetition

Behaviorist Mechanics

Victory / Defeat screens, damage sounds, and large XP numbers immediately after battle completion condition the player with positive feedback via rewards.
